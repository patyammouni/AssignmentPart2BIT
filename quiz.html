Quiz System

The quiz system is designed to be a final test for the user to prove that they understand the lessons in the modules. After finishing a module the user will be guided to a quiz based on that module, as the target audience for the website are young children the quiz will have a simple multiple choice formatting. When the user has submitted their answers for the quiz the system will process these answers and display the results to the user, with the user required to meet a certain amount of correct answers to be awarded a pass for the module. As SafeMe is focused on the education of the user, we will try and ensure that users will not be disheartened by failing marks and will use it as an opportunity to teach the user how to correct their mistakes. The questions for the quiz will be a small randomised subsection of a pool of appropriate questions, this is to ensure that the user is being tested on their actual understanding of the subject and not just their memorisation of the questions.

User Story

Cindy is a 12 year old student, she has been given her first laptop and decided to do some research into internet safety t o keep her laptop safe from malicious software. Finding the SafeMe website, Cindy completes the “Scams and Malicious Software” module and is presented with the module quiz. Drawing upon the knowledge she learned during the module Cindy answers each multiple choice question to the best of her ability and submits her answers. Cindy is then taken to a screen displaying her results, she answered every question correctly and can now obtain her Proof of Achievement for the Scams and Malicious Software module.
